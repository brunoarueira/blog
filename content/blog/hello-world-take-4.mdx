Yeah, you've read it right! This blog is my fourth attempt to come back to write a blog, my previous
attempts were using Blogger, WordPress (the free one, and I didn't host it myself), and
[enki](https://github.com/xaviershay/enki) (an open source Ruby-based blog).

I didn't write much on any of them. I made, I think, two posts at most and then migrated posts
from one platform to another. The previous posts are not worth migrating here, because they're about
other subjects which I don't care too much.

I always thought: if I can learn from others through open source or other sites and blogs,
why can't I give back?

Since we're here, what kind of subject can I give back on? As some of you probably found me through
my site and know a little bit about me, to the others my name is Bruno, I'm a full stack developer
from Brazil working mainly with Ruby and NodeJS (for the backend part), and on the frontend it's a
mix of things (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and React).

Besides that, I like to automate all the things (kind of a DevOps mindset). Some of the things I've done in
the past are: publishing npm packages directly to registries, building Docker images to push into
container registries, and setting up things like receiving Heroku alerts through a bot in a Slack channel.

I've probably written a brief introduction about my experience, and in the next posts I'll expand more
about some of the subjects I've learned.

See you soon!
